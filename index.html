Key AI Technologies

1. Machine Learning

Machine Learning enables systems to learn and improve from experience without explicit programming. It's used in recommendation systems, fraud detection, and more.

2. Natural Language Processing (NLP)

NLP allows computers to understand, interpret, and respond to human language. Applications include chatbots, language translation, and voice assistants like Alexa.

3. Computer Vision

Computer Vision enables machines to interpret and process visual data from the world. It's widely used in facial recognition, medical imaging, and autonomous vehicles.

4. Robotics

Robotics integrates AI to create intelligent robots capable of performing complex tasks, from manufacturing to surgery and space exploration.

5. Generative AI

Generative AI, like GPT, creates new content, such as text, images, or music. It powers chatbots, creative design, and personalized content creation.
