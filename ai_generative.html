Chatbot Information

Understanding the technology behind conversational AI

What is a Chatbot?

A chatbot is a software application designed to simulate human-like conversations with users, typically through text or voice interactions. Chatbots can be rule-based or powered by artificial intelligence.

Types of Chatbots

1. Rule-Based Chatbots

These chatbots follow predefined scripts and rules to respond to user queries. They are simple and work well for straightforward tasks like FAQs.

2. AI-Powered Chatbots

AI chatbots use Natural Language Processing (NLP) and Machine Learning to understand and generate responses. They adapt over time and handle complex interactions.

Applications of Chatbots

  • Customer Support: Automating responses to customer inquiries and resolving common issues.
  • Healthcare: Assisting patients with appointment scheduling and health advice.
  • E-commerce: Guiding customers through purchases, providing recommendations, and offering support.
  • Education: Helping students with learning resources and answering questions.

Benefits of Chatbots

  • Available 24/7 to provide instant support.
  • Reduces the workload on human agents.
  • Improves customer experience with quick and accurate responses.
  • Scalable to handle multiple users simultaneously.
